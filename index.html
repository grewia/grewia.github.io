Our team

GrewIA is currently composed of three world-leading academics and their international networks of active researchers, mentors, tutors and instructors.

Prof. Dr. Stéphane Bordas

Stéphane is an engineer and applied mathematician who teaches and carries out research in computational sciences since year 1999, in various capacities. He has been in the top 1% most cited in his field, worldwide since year 2015. All-in-all, he has taught over 5,000 students directly and given short courses and research seminars reaching thousands of attendees. He has extensive experience in one-to-one tutoring, mentoring and coaching across various disciplines. He has directly worked with supervised over four hundred collaborators and has worked with over fifty different companies, worldwide, as a R&D consultant. Stéphane and his students and collaborators received multiple international prizes for his research. and mentorship. He has raised over 28 million euros in research funding from the private and public sector alike. He is Fellow of the Learned Society of Wales, and recipient of the 2022 Eugenio Beltrami Senior Scientist Prize.

Prof. Dr. Christophe Ley

Christophe is an applied mathematician working in the field of statistics/data science. He is a passionate teacher and presenter, with more than 12 years of experience in teaching statistics, data science and probability courses on various levels, ranging from PhD students in hard sciences (maths, engineering, computer science, …) to first-year Bachelor students in medicine and social science. He is regularly invited to give scientific talks to a general public and has been awarded in 2012 the Wernaers Prize for the dissemination of knowledge by the Belgian National Fund for Scientific Research. In 2014 he won the Prix Marie-Jeanne Laurent-Duhamel for the best PhD thesis in statistics among all French-speaking universities and became an Elected Fellow of the International Statistical Institute. Since 2021 he is President of ECAS, the European Association for Advanced Statistics Courses. Over the past years he has further specialized in collaborating with companies.

Prof. Dr. Ivan Nourdin

Ivan is an applied mathematician whose research focuses mainly on probability theory and statistics. He has been teaching many university courses at all levels, ranging from the most basic (linear algebra, probability and statistics) to the most specialized (Gaussian analysis and applications). Former student of the École Normale Supérieure of Paris-Saclay, he holds the 'agrégation de mathématiques'. He is the author of numerous books, dedicated either to teaching or to research. He won the 2011 Prize of the Foundation of Mathematical Sciences of Paris and the 2015 FNR Award in the category "Outstanding Scientific Publication". His h-index is 39 and his scientific work has been cited over 5,000 times. He has been a member of the jury or referee of more than 40 PhD theses and is regularly invited as a guest speaker all over the world for his innovative work, in particular that on the Malliavin-Stein method or fractional Brownian motion.
